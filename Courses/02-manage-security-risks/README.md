## Overview

The **Manage Security Risks** course is the second step in the Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate program. It teaches learners how to identify, assess, and manage security risks in organizations, which is a critical skill for entry-level cybersecurity roles such as Security Analyst.

## Course Structure

The course follows the Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate curriculum, divided into the following modules:

1. **Introduction to Risk Management**
2. **Risk Assessment Processes**
3. **Threat Modeling**
4. **Vulnerability Management**
5. **Risk Treatment Strategies**
6. **Security Controls Evaluation**
7. **Security Governance and Policies**
8. **Risk Monitoring and Reporting**

## Key Topics

### Introduction to Risk Management
- What is risk management in cybersecurity
- Importance of risk management for organizations
- Risk management frameworks and standards
- Key risk management terminology

### Risk Assessment Processes
- Risk assessment methodologies
- Asset identification and valuation
- Threat assessment and classification
- Vulnerability assessment and prioritization
- Risk calculation and scoring

### Threat Modeling
- Threat modeling concepts and techniques
- Common threat modeling frameworks
- Identifying and prioritizing threats
- Threat mitigation strategies

### Vulnerability Management
- Vulnerability management processes
- Vulnerability scanning and assessment
- Vulnerability prioritization (CVSS scoring)
- Remediation planning and execution
- Verification and validation

### Risk Treatment Strategies
- Risk mitigation techniques
- Risk transfer options (insurance)
- Risk acceptance criteria
- Risk avoidance strategies
- Risk communication

### Security Controls Evaluation
- Security controls classification (preventive, detective, corrective)
- Control effectiveness evaluation
- Control implementation and monitoring
- Control testing and validation
- Control optimization

### Security Governance and Policies
- Security governance frameworks
- Security policies and procedures
- Risk management policies
- Compliance requirements
- Security awareness and training

### Risk Monitoring and Reporting
- Risk monitoring processes
- Key risk indicators (KRIs)
- Risk reporting and communication

- Continuous improvement
